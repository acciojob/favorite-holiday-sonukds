Paris, France


Paris, the capital city of France, is known for its rich history, stunning architecture, and vibrant culture. Often called "The City of Light," Paris is home to world-famous landmarks, museums, and cuisine that attract millions of visitors each year.

  1. Eiffel Tower: The iconic symbol of Paris and a must-visit attraction.
  2. Louvre Museum: The world's largest art museum, home to the Mona Lisa and countless other masterpieces.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral: A Gothic masterpiece with stunning architecture and history.
  4. Sacré-Cœur Basilica: A beautiful basilica located at the highest point in the city, offering breathtaking views.
  5. Champs-Élysées: One of the most famous avenues in the world, known for its theaters, cafés, and luxury shops.
  • Croissants: A must-try flaky and buttery pastry that is quintessentially French.
  • Escargot: A traditional delicacy of snails cooked with garlic and herbs.
  • Crêpes: Thin pancakes that can be filled with a variety of sweet or savory ingredients.
  • Baguettes: Classic French bread, crispy on the outside and soft on the inside.
  • Macarons: Delicate and colorful sandwich cookies with a variety of flavors and fillings.
